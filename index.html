About Me


Hi, I'm Rushabh Soni. I fly planes and write code.


I've been fortunate enough to travel for a living.


I learnt to fly airplanes in New Zealand and lived there for just over 3 years. Following which I started flying the Boeing 737 as a commercial airline pilot at the age of 21, and have been flying ever since. I'm currently fling the A320, been fortunate enough to have flown 3 major commercial airliners so far, including 2 big jets and a turbo-prop.


I hold a bachelor of science degree in Psychology.
